About Our Lab

The Materials Chemistry Research Lab (MCRL) at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Chennai Campus, was established on 23 July 2024 as a dedicated hub for cutting-edge research in advanced materials and sustainable technologies. The lab’s core focus spans energy storage, electrochemistry, carbon capture and conversion, wastewater purification, and precious material purification, driven by innovative approaches in the synthesis and modification of polymers and nanomaterials. MCRL aims to integrate fundamental chemistry with practical engineering solutions, developing novel materials and processes that address critical challenges in clean energy, environmental remediation, and resource recovery, thereby contributing to both scientific advancement and societal benefit.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in chemical research that transforms scientific discoveries into solutions for society's most pressing challenges.

Our Instruments

State-of-the-art equipment enabling cutting-edge research.

Electrochemical Workstation

Used for analyzing electrochemical properties of materials, supporting energy storage and conversion studies.

Glove Box

An inert atmosphere enclosure that allows safe handling of air- and moisture-sensitive materials.

Cell Fabrication Accessories

Specialized equipment for assembling and testing prototype electrochemical cells and devices.

Tubular & Box Furnace

High-temperature furnaces designed for heat treatment, synthesis, and material modification processes.
