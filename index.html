Our Instruments

State-of-the-art equipment enabling cutting-edge research.

HPLC System

High-performance liquid chromatography for precise separation and analysis of complex mixtures.

GC-MS

Gas chromatography-mass spectrometry for volatile compound identification and quantification.

NMR Spectrometer

400 MHz nuclear magnetic resonance spectrometer for molecular structure determination.

UV-Vis Spectrophotometer

Ultraviolet-visible spectroscopy for absorption measurements across a wide wavelength range.

DSC

Differential scanning calorimeter for thermal analysis of materials.

FTIR Spectrometer

Fourier-transform infrared spectroscopy for functional group analysis.
